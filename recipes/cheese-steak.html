Homemade Philly Cheese-steaks

Description:

Philly cheese-steaks are iconicâ€”and for good reason. This is my version of the sandwich, which satisfies my cravings every time.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 4 peppers (a variety of colours), thinly sliced
  • 1 large red onion, sliced
  • 700 g sirloin steak, thinly sliced
  • cheddar cheese
  • 4 baguettes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Mustard

Steps:

  1. In a large pan over medium heat, heat 1 tablespoon oil. Add peppers and onion and season with salt. Cook, stirring often, until caramelised, 12 to 15 minutes.
  2. Remove onions and peppers from pan and set aside. Add remaining tablespoon oil and cook steak until it has almost reached your preferred doneness, about 5 minutes.
  3. Season with salt, pepper and a healthy dose of mustard.
  4. Return vegetables to pan and toss to combine with steak.
  5. Lightly toast the baguettes in your oven
  6. Grate cheese over mixture and cook, covered until the cheese is melted, about 3 minutes more.
  7. Divide mixture among baguettes and serve.
