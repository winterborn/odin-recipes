Gimlet Cocktail

Description:

A classic gimlet doesn’t require much: gin (or vodka, if you must), lime, and simple syrup. That’s it! It’s a perfectly balanced gin sour that’s equally appropriate for a dinner party or a casual night at home.

Ingredients:

  • 50ml lime syrup or lime cordial
  • ice
  • 50ml Sipsmith Gin
  • slice of lime and an edible flower, to garnish

Steps:

  1. Put a martini or coupe glass in the fridge to chill.
  2. Pour 50ml of the lime syrup or cordial into a jug or tall glass and add a few ice cubes and the gin. Stir until the outside of the container feels very cold.
  3. Strain the mixture into your chilled glass and garnish with a slice of lime and an edible flower.
